The export-control directive that suspended Fable 5 and Mythos 5 globally on June 12 still stands, but this is the clearest presidential signal yet of openness to restoration. Prediction markets: 57% before July 1, 75% before July 17.

**June 22 is tomorrow.** The formal meeting between Anthropic executives and the Commerce Department / National Cyber Director — Anthropic's first in-person venue with the officials who issued the directive — is scheduled for June 22. This is where a framework for selective restoration (US customers first) would be negotiated. Watch [anthropic.com/news](https://www.anthropic.com/news) for the authoritative signal.

**John Jumper joins Anthropic.** On June 19, John Jumper — co-creator of AlphaFold, the protein-structure AI that earned him the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry — announced he is leaving Google DeepMind after nine years. His specific role at Anthropic is undisclosed. The hire tracks Anthropic's 2026 AI-for-science buildout (wet labs, Allen Institute and HHMI partnerships). The second major talent departure from DeepMind in a week — Noam Shazeer also left for OpenAI the same week.

**Why it matters:** Trump's shift is the most significant political signal since the ban; the June 22 meeting is the first formal venue where a restoration deal could be struck. Jumper's arrival signals Anthropic is building a serious scientific research track alongside its developer tools.
