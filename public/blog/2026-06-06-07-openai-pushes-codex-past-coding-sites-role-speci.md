In a [June 2 update](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/openais-codex-update-lets-agents-build-interactive-enterprise-workspaces-via-sites-and-role-specific-plugins), OpenAI repositioned Codex from a programming assistant into a general work environment: "Sites" lets agents build and share hosted dashboards/apps via URL, six role-specific plugins bundle 62 business apps (Snowflake, Figma, Salesforce) and 110 skills, and "Annotations" allows targeted edits to docs/sites without regenerating everything. Notably, non-developers are now ~20% of Codex's 5M weekly users and adopting it 3× faster than engineers. For software teams this signals the agent surface expanding well beyond the IDE — and more non-engineers shipping internal tooling you may end up maintaining.
