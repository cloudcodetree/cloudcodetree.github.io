A sobering data point making the rounds: despite 84% of developers using AI tools daily, measured productivity gains average only 10%. A METR controlled study found experienced developers actually took 19% *longer* on tasks when using AI. Root causes: over-reliance without review, poor context management, and deployment problems from untested AI-generated code. The implication for your workflow: the gains are real for teams with strong testing, review practices, and context hygiene — but not automatic.
