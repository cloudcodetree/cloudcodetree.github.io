Google announced that OpenAI, Kakao, and ElevenLabs are adopting **SynthID**, its watermarking standard for AI-generated content. This nudges watermarking toward an industry default — relevant if you build anything that ingests, moderates, or republishes generated images/audio/text, since you can increasingly rely on a shared provenance signal rather than rolling your own detection.
