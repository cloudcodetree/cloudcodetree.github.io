Apple confirmed that Siri AI's new personal-context and assistant features **will not ship in the EU at launch** for iOS 27 and iPadOS 27, citing Digital Markets Act compliance work.

For engineers building App Intents integrations or features that hook into Siri AI capabilities, this is a concrete reminder that AI feature availability is now **region-gated by regulation**. Design for graceful degradation and test the EU configuration as a first-class path, not an afterthought — "available everywhere" is no longer a safe default for AI features.

**Sources:** [Apple Newsroom: Siri AI delayed in EU](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2026/06/due-to-dma-siri-ai-delayed-in-eu-for-ios-27-and-ipados-27/)
